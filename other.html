Koggala Turtle Hatchery
The Koggala Turtle Hatchery is located on the southern coast of Sri Lanka and has been in operation since 1996. The hatchery focuses on the conservation of sea turtles and also offers a variety of educational programs for visitors.

Induruwa Sea Turtle Conservation Project
The Induruwa Sea Turtle Conservation Project is located on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka and is dedicated to conserving and protecting sea turtles. The hatchery offers educational programs and volunteer opportunities for visitors, and also collaborates with local schools and communities to raise awareness about sea turtle conservation.

Kosgoda Sea Turtle Conservation Association
The Kosgoda Sea Turtle Conservation Association is a non-profit organization dedicated to conserving and protecting sea turtles along the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka. The organization operates a turtle hatchery and offers guided tours and educational programs for visitors.

Reptile Village Turtle Conservation Project
The Reptile Village Turtle Conservation Project is located in the southern part of Sri Lanka and is dedicated to conserving and protecting sea turtles. The hatchery offers guided tours and educational programs for visitors, as well as other conservation efforts for reptiles.

Beruwala Turtle Conservation Project
The Beruwala Turtle Conservation Project is located on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka and focuses on conserving sea turtles, particularly the Green turtle. The hatchery offers visitors guided tours and educational programs and collaborates with local communities to raise awareness about sea turtle conservation. Kahanda Kanda Turtle Hatchery The Kahanda Kanda Turtle Hatchery is located on the southern coast of Sri Lanka and is dedicated to conserving and protecting sea turtles. The hatchery offers guided tours and educational programs for visitors and also operates a turtle rescue centre.
